Facial Wash – 150ml bottle with dispenser

A gentle foaming facial wash in an ideal combination of Aloe and other active ingredients that helps reduce sebum flow, restores the skin balance, calm and sooth the skin, and assist in refining the texture to help reveal skin’s natural radiance, all without harming the skin’s natural oil balance.Read More

Application

Apply to a damp face and gently massage into the skin with fingertips, rinse off with lukewarm water – can also be used as an eye make-up remover. Can be used on Problematic or Combination skin types. The Aloe Unique Facial Wash will leave the skin radiant, refined and perfectly balanced. Use in the morning and evenings, before toning and moisturizing.
