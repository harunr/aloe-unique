Our mission & values

Our Mission:

To ethically and sustainably build our brand and produce the highest quality of affordable health and skin care products.

We combine the best of ancient and modern technology, blending vitamins with ecologically-harvested Aloe Ferox plant ingredients, preserving with a paraben-free formula and packing our high-quality range in recyclable containers.

Our core values:
  • 1
    Maintaining a high level of ethics, integrity and honesty
  • 2
    Building mutually beneficial relationships and helping the community
  • 2
    Producing world class products that are environmentally friendly

Our heritage

We are a family-run business with over 20 years’ experience in the aloe industry in South Africa. We are passionate about enterprise, and are proud to be the first company in South Africa to combine vitamins with an Aloe-based skin care range. We care deeply about health and the environment; all of our products are paraben-free and we use recyclable containers.

As a family business we prioritize trust and respect and pride ourselves on community involvement, sustainability and good relationships. We have strong connections in Africa, and are creating links with markets in China, USA and Europe. With a wealth of experience in the aloe industry we are aiming to make our affordable and high-quality products available worldwide.

Aloe Unique Features

We use the Cape Aloe Ferox plant ingredient, harvested using ancient methods by local South Africans. This creates jobs and a sustainable future for the community and the environment.

Safe for your skin: Aloe Unique is the only skin care range to combine rejuvenating vitamins with the age-defying properties of Aloe Ferox. Aloe Unique has a mild natural perfume, therefore ideal for sensitive skin. All our products are paraben free. Our products are also alcohol free and non-poisonous.

Safe for the environment: The Aloe Ferox plant grows wild without pesticides or chemicals. We use recyclable containers and our products are not tested on animals.
